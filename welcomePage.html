You refused to leave your house once it foreclosed. Now you'll do anything to get your house back, including living in the walls and convincing the current residents that the house is haunted.

Player Movement

  • W or Arrow Up :
    Move Player Up
  • A or Arrow Left :
    Move Player Up
  • S or Arrow Down :
    Move Player Down
  • D or Arrow Right:
    Move Sprite Right
  • Q:
    Enter and Exit Through Paintings or Change Floors
  • E:
    Interact With Furniture
  • R:
    Drag and Drop Items
  • Z:
    Interact with Special Items
  • Click the buttons in the text area to progress in quest dialog, or to add items to the inventory
